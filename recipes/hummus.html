Hummus

Description

Hummus is easy

Let the chickpeas do the work

Ingredients

  • One can of Chickpeas
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Lime
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Rinse and fry Chickpeas with spices of your choice
  2. Add chickpeas to container with chopped garlic olive salt and cumin
  3. Blend together, add water if necessary
  4. Add salt, cumin , or olive oil to taste. Blend until you find the right balance.
