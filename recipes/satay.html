Satay Sauce

Description

This recipe is to create a satay sauce to accompany your nutrition bowls

Feel free to add some extra spices into the mix

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Curry Powder
  • Soy Sauce
  • Fish Sauce
  • Coconut Milk
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Mix everything except for the coconut milk in a bowl
  2. Heat coconut milk in a saucepan and mix with the other ingredients
