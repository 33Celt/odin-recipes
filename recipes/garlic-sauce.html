Garlic Sauce

Description

This garlic sauce is to use up your aquafaba

Feel free to add some extra spices into the mix

Ingredients

  • Garlic ( 7Cloves)
  • Aquafaba 200ml
  • Lime 1
  • salt

Steps

  1. Press garlic and juice lime into a container with the aquafaba and salt
  2. blend until emulsified
